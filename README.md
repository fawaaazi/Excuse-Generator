### Project Description
Absurd & Blender Excuse Generator Using AI

### The Problem (that doesn't exist)
Society suffers from a crippling surplus of plausibility. Our daily explanations are shackled by logic and verifiable facts.
### The Solution (that nobody asked for)
The AI Absurd Excuse Generator is a creative tool designed to inject surrealism into everyday life. It operates on a principle of "Narrative Destabilization," generating logic-defying scenarios to serve as explanations for common situations like missing a deadline or being late.
